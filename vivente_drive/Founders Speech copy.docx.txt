Founder’s Speech – Vivian of Viventé


Hello everyone!


I’m Vivian, founder of Viventé—a company that builds homes, landscapes dreams, and makes swimming pools so beautiful you’ll want to live in them (but please don’t, we still recommend a house).


Let me tell you how this all started. One day, I looked at a backyard and thought, “This could be more than a patch of grass and a lonely garden gnome.” And just like that, Viventé was born—with a mission to turn ordinary spaces into extraordinary experiences.


We specialize in:


• Swimming pools that make you say “wow” before you even dip a toe in
• Landscaping that makes your neighbors suspiciously friendly
• Home construction that’s built to last and designed to impress
• And maintenance and premium pool products that keep everything looking fabulous (because even pools need a little self-care)




At Viventé, we don’t just build—we beautify. We don’t just maintain—we pamper. And we don’t just sell products—we deliver poolside happiness in a box.


Our team is made up of dreamers, doers, and people who know the difference between a shovel and a spade (trust me, it matters). We’re here to make your space feel like a five-star resort—even if it’s just your backyard.


So whether you want a pool that sparkles, a garden that blooms, or a home that hugs you back, Viventé is ready to make it happen—with style, substance, and maybe a few dad jokes along the way.


Thank you for trusting us. Let’s build something beautiful—and maybe throw a pool party while we’re at it.


Warmly,
Vivian
Founder, Viventé
