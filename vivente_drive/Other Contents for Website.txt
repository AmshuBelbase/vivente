What We Do
At Vivente, we create environments that embody elegance, innovation, and timeless living. Our integrated expertise spans architecture, interiors, outdoor landscapes, wellness experiences, and real estate, allowing us to craft complete lifestyle destinations with uncompromising quality.
Construction

Interior Designing & Execution
Our bespoke interior design services transform spaces into refined expressions of luxury and comfort. From concept development to complete turnkey execution, we curate every detail to create interiors that are elegant, functional, and uniquely reflective of each client’s lifestyle.
Swimming Pools
Vivente designs and builds exceptional swimming pools that elevate outdoor living. Whether it is a private residential retreat, a stunning infinity-edge pool, or a large-scale commercial installation, our pools combine advanced engineering, sophisticated design, and premium finishes.
Landscaping
Our landscaping services bring nature and architecture together in perfect harmony. Through thoughtful garden design, curated plant selections, and expertly crafted outdoor environments, we transform landscapes into serene, resort-inspired spaces of beauty and tranquility.
Water Features
Water has the power to transform a space. Vivente creates bespoke water features including fountains, waterfalls, ponds, and flowing streams that introduce movement, elegance, and a calming sensory experience to architectural environments.
Water Wellness
We design luxurious wellness environments that inspire relaxation and rejuvenation. Our offerings include saunas, steam rooms, hot tubs, swim spas, and custom wellness retreat concepts that bring the experience of a world-class spa into private and commercial settings.
Real Estate & Property Consultancy

AMC & Facility Management
Our commitment extends far beyond project completion. Through professional Annual Maintenance and Facility Management services, we ensure every property, installation, and environment continues to operate flawlessly, preserving both performance and long-term value.
