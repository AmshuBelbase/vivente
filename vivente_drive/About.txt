About Viventé


At Viventé, we believe that living beautifully is an art. Founded by Vivian, our company was built on the vision of creating spaces that inspire, endure, and elevate everyday life.


From swimming pool design and maintenance to landscaping that transforms outdoor environments, and home construction crafted with precision and trust, Viventé stands at the intersection of luxury and functionality. We also offer a curated line of premium swimming pool products, ensuring that every detail of your space reflects elegance and quality.


Our Philosophy


The name Viventé embodies vitality, sophistication, and timeless living. It represents our commitment to blending artistry with engineering, nature with nurture, and design with discipline. Every project we undertake is guided by integrity, creativity, and an unwavering pursuit of excellence.


What We Do


• Swimming Pools: Bespoke designs, expert maintenance, and premium products.
• Landscaping: Transforming outdoor spaces into lush sanctuaries.
• Construction: Building homes that balance beauty, durability, and functionality.




Our Promise


At Viventé, we don’t just build structures—we craft experiences. We don’t just maintain pools—we preserve beauty. And we don’t just design landscapes—we create legacies.


With Vivian’s leadership and a team of dedicated professionals, Viventé is committed to delivering projects that embody luxury, precision, and trust.
